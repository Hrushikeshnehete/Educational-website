Maths Guide

Subjective
  • Read the Question Carefully: Understand what's being asked before you begin solving.
  • Identify Given Data: List out all the information and values provided in the question.
  • State the Required Formula: Write the relevant formula or theorem.
Show Step-by-Step
  1. Step 1: Substitute the given data into the formula.
  2. Step 2: Simplify the equation or expression step by step.
  3. Step 3: Clearly mention intermediate steps and calculations.
  4. Final Answer: Highlight or box the final answer for clarity.
  5. Verification (if applicable): Verify the solution for accuracy.
Objective Questions
  • Understand the Question: Read the problem carefully to identify what's being asked.
  • Option Elimination: Eliminate incorrect options in multiple-choice questions.
  • Quick Calculation: Perform necessary calculations or mental math.
  • Choose the Correct Option: Clearly mark the correct answer.
General Tips
  • Neatness Counts: Write neatly and legibly for clarity.
  • Time Management: Be mindful of time, avoid spending too long on a single question.
  • Double-Check: Review your answers if time permits, especially for errors.
