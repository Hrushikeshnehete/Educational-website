11th Important Topics

Physics

Physical World and Measurement: Understanding the scope of physics, units of measurement, and fundamental forces of nature.
uKinematics: Motion in a straight line, motion in a plane, and laws of motion.
Work, Energy, and Power: Concepts of work, energy, and power, and their applications.
Thermodynamics: Laws of thermodynamics, heat, and temperature.
Waves and Oscillations: Types of waves, wave motion, and sound.
Chemistry

Some Basic Concepts of Chemistry: Nature of matter, properties of matter, and laws of chemical combination.
Structure of Atom: Atomic models, discovery of sub-atomic particles, and dual behavior of electromagnetic radiation.
Chemical Bonding and Molecular Structure: Types of chemical bonds, molecular orbital theory, and hybridization.
Thermodynamics: Enthalpy, entropy, and Gibbs free energy.
Equilibrium: Chemical equilibrium, Le Chatelier's principle, and ionic equilibrium.
Biology

Diversity of Living Organisms: Classification of living organisms, plant kingdom, and animal kingdom.
Structural Organization in Plants and Animals: Morphology and anatomy of flowering plants, and structural organization in animals.
Cell Structure and Function: Cell theory, cell organelles, and cell cycle.
Plant Physiology: Photosynthesis, respiration, and plant growth.
Human Physiology: Digestion, respiration, circulation, and excretion.
